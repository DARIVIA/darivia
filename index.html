Decision Trajectory Intelligence (DTI)

A framework for identifying mismatches between products and customers
through AI-assisted selection.
Reveals which product characteristics do not match customer goals, preferences and parameters.

PROBLEM
“Doesn’t work” isn’t an answer.
Brands see outcomes — sell-through, returns, markdowns.
But customer-product mismatches remain largely invisible.
·  What doesn’t match.
·  Which customers it doesn’t match.
·  Where mismatches occur.
GAP
Every day, customers evaluate products through conversations, search, filtering, recommendations, and increasingly through AI-assisted selection.

But brands rarely capture these evaluations as structured data.
MARKET SHIFT
Product selection is becoming increasingly AI-assisted.
For the first time, customer-product fit can be evaluated and recorded at scale.
This creates a new source of merchandising intelligence that previously did not exist.
